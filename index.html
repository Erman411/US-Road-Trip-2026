Permit & Booking Alerts

⚠️
Zion NP / Springdale · Jul 24

Very limited gateway town; the in-park lodge is unique and sells out months ahead. Book refundable rates now.

⚠️
Yosemite National Park · Aug 2

Summer lodging is extremely competitive. Tioga Pass (needed from Mammoth) opened May 15, 2026 — well ahead of your dates, so access isn't a concern this year.

⚠️
Monument Valley · Jul 25

Limited lodging on the whole route; view rooms and cabins at The View Hotel go fast.

⚠️
Grand Canyon S. Rim / Williams · Jul 27

South Rim lodging fills well in advance in summer — El Tovar can sell out a year ahead.

⚠️
Bryce Canyon NP · Jul 23

Small lodging market near the park — the in-park Lodge is the one to chase first.

⚠️
Denver / Boulder · Jul 17–19

World Cup Final city base — good LoDo/downtown locations will tighten as the date approaches. Reserve Rocky Mountain NP timed entry for the Jul 18 day trip if entering during the reservation window (especially Bear Lake Road).

⚠️
Lake Tahoe / Reno · Aug 3–4

Summer Tahoe lodging can get expensive and limited — book early.

⚠️
Mammoth Lakes · Aug 1

Small mountain town and Yosemite gateway — limited inventory in peak summer.

⚠️
Scottsdale / Old Town · Jul 29–30

Two nights — lock a good Old Town location while July's off-peak desert pricing lasts.

⚠️
Moab · Jul 21–22

Limited strong hotel inventory near Arches/Canyonlands. Arches itself requires timed-entry tickets May–September on recreation.gov.

⚠️
Shenandoah NP / Luray · Jul 6

Inside-park lodging (Skyland, Big Meadows) is limited in summer — book immediately.
